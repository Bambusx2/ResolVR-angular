About the Project

Professor Gorgieva sought to modernize her piano teaching methods through digital technology integration. Our collaboration focused on developing a comprehensive platform that would enhance both teaching efficiency and student learning experience.

The Challenge

The piano school needed a unified system to manage sheet music, track student progress, and provide interactive learning tools. The challenge was to create an intuitive platform that would preserve the personal teaching approach while leveraging digital advantages.

Our Solution

We developed a piano learning platform that combines interactive lessons, progress tracking, and direct communication with the professor. The solution enables personalized learning paths, real-time feedback, and comprehensive music education resources.
