About the Project

Rima-Term, established in 1995, has grown into a leading producer of sunflower oil, seeds, and agricultural products. Our collaboration began in 2022 with the goal of modernizing their warehouse and production management processes.

The Challenge

The challenge was to create a unified system that could handle their diverse product range while maintaining precise inventory control across multiple warehouses. Our solution streamlined their operations from raw material procurement to finished product distribution.

Our Solution

We developed a custom warehouse management system with integrated production planning capabilities, focusing on user-friendly interfaces and robust backend architecture. The solution was designed to scale with the company's growth and adapt to changing business requirements.
