About the Project

The University of Goce Delchev sought to modernize their mineral research capabilities through digital transformation. Our collaboration focused on developing a comprehensive platform for mineral exploration and analysis.

The Challenge

The university needed a unified system to manage mineral data, research findings, and analysis tools. The challenge was to create an intuitive platform that would streamline research processes while providing powerful analytical capabilities.

Our Solution

We developed a mineral research platform that combines data management, analysis tools, and visualization capabilities. The solution enables efficient mineral research, data analysis, and collaboration among researchers.
