Project Overview

Hotel Park is a next-generation hotel management system designed to streamline operations and enhance the guest experience. The project aimed to provide a seamless, intelligent, and automated platform for managing hotels, room reservations, and energy consumption.

Project Scope

Our team collaborated with Hotel Park to build a feature-rich hotel management system from scratch. The project required a cross-functional team of designers, backend and frontend developers, and QA engineers.

Key Challenges

  • Integrating multiple IoT devices for smart room control
  • Building a scalable and highly available backend infrastructure
  • Designing an intuitive and user-friendly mobile interface
  • Optimizing energy management and automation features
