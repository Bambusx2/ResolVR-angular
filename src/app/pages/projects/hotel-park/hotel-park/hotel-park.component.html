About the Project

Hotel Park sought to modernize their operations through smart technology integration. Our collaboration focused on developing a comprehensive system that would enhance both operational efficiency and guest experience.

The Challenge

The hotel needed a unified system to manage room automation, energy consumption, and guest services. The challenge was to create an intuitive platform that would seamlessly integrate various IoT devices while providing powerful management tools.

Our Solution

We developed a smart hotel management system that combines IoT integration, mobile accessibility, and comprehensive analytics. The solution enables automated room control, energy optimization, and real-time monitoring of hotel operations.
