About the Project

CityGym wanted to enhance member engagement and retention through a custom mobile application. Our collaboration focused on developing a comprehensive fitness tracking solution that would align with their brand and training philosophy.

The Challenge

The gym needed a way to digitize their workout programs and create a stronger community among members. The challenge was to create an intuitive app that would appeal to users of all fitness levels while providing valuable data to the gym.

Our Solution

We developed a custom fitness app with workout tracking, goal setting, and community features. The solution includes leaderboards, progress visualization, and social sharing capabilities, resulting in increased member engagement and retention.
